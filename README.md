# 🤖 AI Library

## 📌 Overview

This repository is a structured and organized library of artificial intelligence concepts, designed to provide a clear overview of the different types and categories of AI. It brings together key areas of artificial intelligence into a single directory, making it easier to explore, understand, and navigate the field without confusion.

Each category is grouped logically and includes concise descriptions explaining its purpose, functionality, and real-world relevance. The goal is to simplify complex AI topics into an accessible and beginner-friendly format.

---

## 🧠 What’s Inside

This library covers major branches of AI, including:

* **Machine Learning** – Systems that learn from data and improve over time
* **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** – AI that understands and generates human language
* **Computer Vision** – AI that processes and interprets visual data
* **Types of AI** – Including Narrow AI, General AI, and Super AI

Each section is organized for clarity and easy navigation.
